Who We Are

FatalPast is a premier digital archive dedicated to the exploration and documentation of historical criminal cases, unsolved mysteries, and the darker side of human history. Founded by a team of historians, researchers, and true crime enthusiasts, we aim to provide a comprehensive, educational, and respectful platform for analyzing the events that have shaped forensic science and societal understanding of crime.

Our database is meticulously curated using primary sources, archival police records, and expert historical analysis. We believe that by understanding the past—no matter how dark—we can better comprehend the evolution of justice and the human psyche.

Educational Focus

We prioritize accuracy and educational value above all else. Every vaka (case) in our archive is presented with historical context, forensic details, and verified facts.

Community Driven

Our 5,000+ strong community on Discord and social media platforms actively participates in discussions, theory development, and historical research.
